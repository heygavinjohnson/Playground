Frequently asked questions

Start by searching for agents in your specific suburb using our search tool above. Look for agents with:

  • High ratings and positive reviews from past clients
  • Recent sales in your area and property type
  • Local market expertise and knowledge
  • Professional credentials and experience

Our platform shows you verified agents with proven track records in your area.

When choosing an agent, consider these key factors:

  • Local expertise: Do they know your market well?
  • Communication style: Do you feel comfortable working with them?
  • Marketing strategy: How will they promote your property?
  • Track record: What are their recent sales results?
  • Fees and commission: Are their rates competitive?

Interview multiple agents and ask for references from recent clients.

You can contact agents through our platform in several ways:

  • Click "Contact" on any agent profile to send a message
  • Use our "Connect with a local agent" form above
  • Call agents directly using their listed phone numbers
  • Visit their agency office in person

Most agents respond within 24 hours and offer free initial consultations.

To leave a review for an agent:

  • Visit the agent's profile page
  • Click "Write a Review" or "Rate this Agent"
  • Rate their service (1-5 stars)
  • Write a detailed review about your experience
  • Submit your review for moderation

Reviews help other property owners make informed decisions and help agents improve their services.
